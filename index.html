About

Kerala is located on the southwestern coast of India. This Indian state is a land of breathtaking natural beauty, vibrant culture, and serene backwaters. It is known as God’s own country. Kerala casts a spell on the tourist with its innate beauty and ethereal charm. Kerala’s lush greenery, tranquil beaches, misty hill stations, exotic wildlife, and cultural richness is a must-see. We have curated the list of places to visit in Kerala. It is perfect destination for couples, nature lovers, family vacationers and adventure seekers.
